Open Data Train Feeds

Welcome to the Open Data Train Feed, developed by First Great Western IT. This online service delivers bite sized chunks of JSON data for mobile, tablet and desktop apps

Data provided by Network Rail under license

Powered by NODE.JS, JQuery and Bootstrap
